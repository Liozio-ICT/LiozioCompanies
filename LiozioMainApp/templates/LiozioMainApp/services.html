Agricultural Solutions : Liozio Farms & Foods - LFF

Transforming agriculture through innovation and sustainability.

Liozio Farms focuses on sustainable animal farming and research-driven agricultural practices. They offer high-quality livestock including chicken, turkey, cows, goats, fish, and pigs.

The farm is a model for addressing SDG2 - Zero Hunger, contributing to local food security through innovative farming techniques and research collaboration with the University of Ibadan.

Automobile Care Solutions-JOOMBOW

The Liozio Solutions - TLS

Elevating Automobile care with premium, accessible services

TLS, under the brand JOOMBOW, provides comprehensive car care services including repair, rental, and washing. The service is designed to offer affordable, high-quality automotive solutions, aiming to improve community well-being and align with SDG3 - Good Health and Well-Being.

Real Estate Development

Liozio Real Estate Company - LREC

Crafting modern living spaces that foster community and sustainability.

LREC focuses on developing residential and mixed-use properties that promote sustainable and communal living. The Agnes project is an example, offering 3-floor, 4-bedroom terrace duplexes aimed at enhancing the standard of living in Ibadan. This aligns with SDG11 - Sustainable Cities and Communities.

Innovation and Startup Technology

Liozio Innovation & Startup Technology - LIST:

Empowering entrepreneurs with resources and opportunities for growth

LIST is a startup hub dedicated to nurturing entrepreneurs and fostering innovation. It provides resources, training, and funding for startup founders, helping them grow their businesses. The hub aims to create job opportunities and economic growth, supporting SDG8 - Decent Work and Economic Growth.
